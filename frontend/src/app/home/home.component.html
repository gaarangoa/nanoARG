About nanoARG

An online pipeline for the analysis of antimicrobial resistance. That includes to screen the nanopore reads agains Antibiotic Resistance Genes (ARGs), Metal Resistance Genes (MRGs), Mobile Genetic Elements (MGEs) that help to the spread of ARGs/MGEs, and taxonomy annotation that allows to understand the context of the nanopore reads.

  • DeepARG is used to annotate ARGs
  • A set of collected MGEs from NCBI
  • BacMet for the metal resistance profiling
  • Centrifuge is used to infer the taxonomy of each nanopore read

Our recent projects

We have been actively developing bioinformatics solutions. Particularly for the analysis of antimicrobial resistance on Metagenomes. Here there is a list of our recent developed tools.

  • DeepARG: A deep learning approach to predict antibiotic resistance on metagenomes.
  • ARGminer: A crowdsourcing based approach for the annotation and inspection of antibiotic resistance genes
  • MetaStorm: A public webservice for the analysis of metagenomes.
